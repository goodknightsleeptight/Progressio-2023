OUR AIMS

  • We have several aims in our mission to solving climate change.

  • Firstly, to understand the broader picture of climate change.

  • Secondly, to raise awareness on the severity of climate change

  • Thirdly, to provide environmental justice (give back to mother earth)

  • Lastly, to ensure the preservation of earth for future life.


    • As we satisfy these goals and reach our targets, we can board the right train towards improving climate change and saving the environment.
