About UNSDG 13: CLIMATE ACTION

UN Sustainable Development Goal 13 (SDG 13) is focused on "Climate Action." It calls for immediate and coordinated efforts to combat climate change and its impacts. SDG 13 aims to strengthen resilience to climate-related disasters, implement measures to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and promote sustainable practices. This goal is critical in addressing the urgent global need to mitigate climate change and protect the environment for future generations.
